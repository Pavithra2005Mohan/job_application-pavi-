Center for Wireless Networks and Applications invite you to the first Webinar Of 2021 on today's hot Topic "Technology , Transition and Transformation- Towards 5G OpenRAN" Join us on this Sunday - Januray 10, 2021 : 7.00 PM To 8.00 PM IST. The event is Open to All and Free. You can Register Online Here : http://bit.ly/Amrita5G2021 Scope of The Webinar Today 5G is going through OpenRAN transformation in the aspects of architecture, implementation and deployment. Telecom and IT vendors have been developing platform solutions towards 5G network deployments. To avoid vendor lock-in, service providers have shown keen interest in deploying the open source solutions and influencing the ongoing open source initiatives. OpenRAN forms part of this framework. The key challenges for both cellular service providers (CSP) and equipment vendors is to ensure different network elements in ran to work effectively to support three use cases of eMBB (enhanced mobile broadband), URLLC (ultra reliable low latency communications), mMTC (massive machine type communications). This live webinar that will dig into the varied and various aspects of openran landscape including system design considerations, open source standards, protocols, interfaces etc. We will highlight the latest industrial and research opportunities in OpenRAN. Resource Persons - Dr. Seshaiah Ponnekanti, (Adjunct Faculty - Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham) Sree Lekshmi S (Research Scholar, Center For Wireless Networks And Applications) Rekha M Das( Graduate Student, Center For Wireless Networks And Applications) This webinar will be conducted online via Zoom and the link will be shared upon Registration.
