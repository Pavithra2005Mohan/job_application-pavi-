Job Description

Collaborating with stakeholders to gather and analyze user requirements, and translate them into creative and visually appealing designs within the WordPress framework. Designing custom WordPress themes, layouts, graphics, and user interfaces tailored to meet the needs of our diverse audience. Ensuring the usability and accessibility of WordPress designs across various devices and browsers. Implementing responsive design principles to optimize website performance and user experience on mobile devices within the WordPress platform. Working closely with content creators and developers to ensure the seamless integration of design elements into WordPress pages. Staying updated on industry trends and best practices in web design, user experience, and accessibility standards within the WordPress ecosystem. Providing ongoing maintenance and support for existing WordPress websites, including updating and optimizing design elements as needed. Documenting design specifications, processes, and best practices to facilitate collaboration within the team.

About Employer

Established in 1999, Star Entertainment is a globally recognized production company known for its contributions to film, live entertainment, culture, and philanthropy. From investments in blockbuster films like "Shaft" and "Mission Impossible II" to collaborations with esteemed talents such as Richard Curtis and Oscar-winning individuals, Star Entertainment has made a lasting impact on the industry. Our cultural projects span collaborations with initiatives like Bob Geldof's "Live 8," the creation of "The Wall Museum" in Berlin, and installations featuring renowned artists like Ai Weiwei. We've hosted legendary figures including Sting, Liza Minelli, Bryan Adams, Anna Netrebko, Lang Lang, Bono, Roger Waters, and Arcade Fire. In philanthropy, we've organized the annual Cinema for Peace Gala since 2002, initiated "Sports for Peace" with icons like Muhammad Ali and Nelson Mandela, and launched "Help Haiti Home" with Sean Penn, raising over 50 million USD for charitable causes.

Qualifications

Proven experience in web design with a strong portfolio showcasing your creative work, specifically within the WordPress platform. Proficiency in design tools such as Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, XD) or Sketch. Familiarity with WordPress themes, plugins, and page builders such as Elementor or WPBakery. Knowledge of responsive design principles and experience with optimizing WordPress websites for mobile devices. Creative problem-solving skills and attention to detail in design and user experience within the WordPress environment. Ability to work collaboratively in a fast-paced environment and meet project deadlines. Strong communication skills and the ability to present and articulate design concepts to stakeholders. Passion for the entertainment industry and a desire to contribute to projects that make a meaningful impact.
